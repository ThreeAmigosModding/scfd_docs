# Advanced Garbage Job [#advanced-garbage-job]

SCFD GarbageJob is a fully dynamic and performance-optimized garbage collection job for FiveM, designed for realism, replay-ability, and server economy integration.

Players can clock in, receive dynamically assigned trash zones across Los Santos and Blaine County, collect and unload trash, and earn configurable payouts with optional loot rewards.
Each truck tracks its own progress, ensuring that every run feels unique and balanced for both solo and multi-player crews.

## Key Features: [#key-features]

* 🚛 Truck-Based Progression: Trash collection is tied directly to the truck, not the player - perfect for multi-person crews.
* 👥 Multiplayer Groups: Players can create groups and invite their friends to participate in jobs with them.
* 🗺️ Dynamic Zone System: Automatically assigns random zones based on cooldowns, distance, and truck capability.
* ♻️ Configurable Trash Types: Supports bags, bins, and dumpsters with unique yield ranges.
* 💰 Custom Rewards & Payouts: Add item drops, cash bonuses, or rarity-based loot pools per zone.
* 🔁 Zone Cooldowns: Prevent repetitive routes and encourage players to explore the map.
* ⚙️ Lightweight & Optimized: Built with performance, stability and scalability in mind.
* 🧩 Fully Configurable: All data-driven via easily editable config and zone files.

## Integration & Customization [#integration--customization]

* Compatible with all major frameworks (QB, Qbox, ND, ESX) or works standalone.
* Supports any\* inventory, target system, notifications, progressbars & skillchecks.
* Bridge included with hooks to add your own integrations (ie, when trash is added to a truck, when a player is clocked in and out & more)
* Detailed configuration files, allowing you to customize anything you need.
* Full localization supporting any language, pre-configured for English out of the box.

-# \* &#x2A;Only includes ox\_inventory, ox\_target, and ox\_lib by default. You will need to integrate your own and be sure it supports the required features.*
